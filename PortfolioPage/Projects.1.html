About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Nicholas Knight, and I'm passionate about business management and technology. With a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration Management from St. Petersburg College, I've cultivated a unique skill set that merges my love for business strategy with my expertise in programming and web development. I just recently updated this portfolio site; however, the projects within this site still need to be updated. For now I am filling out my portfolio layout with my old projects.

Growing up in an environment steeped in IT and business management, I've honed my abilities by creating numerous queries, websites, and e-commerce stores using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, I've been actively involved in the JavaScript programming community, collaborating on projects ranging from gaming applications to web development solutions. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to manage teams across various fields, leveraging my leadership skills to drive success and foster collaboration. My approach to management is rooted in effective communication, strategic thinking, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

The inspiration behind this portfolio website stems from my desire to showcase my work and skill sets to potential employers and clients. By providing a glimpse into my projects and capabilities, I aim to create a "wow factor" that highlights my creativity, technical proficiency, and commitment to excellence. Outside of my professional pursuits, you'll often find me indulging in my personal hobbies, which largely revolve around exploring new technologies and learning new skills. With an outgoing personality and a relentless drive to excel, I'm always eager to tackle new challenges and exceed expectations.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I look forward to connecting with you and exploring opportunities for collaboration and growth.
